Intro to VR with Unity

November 1, 2024 | 4:00 PM - 6:00 PM
In Person: AVW3258

Have you dreamed of making your own virtual reality game? In this workshop, we cover the basics of creating a virtual-reality application within Unity. Learn why Unity is utilized for virtual-reality games, how to set up a basic scene, and create simple interactions within. Towards the end, we will build a live demo that utilizes the concepts that we will have demonstrated. If you would like to follow along, bring your laptop and make sure that a Unity 2022 or higher is installed!

Lab Meet + Shader Graph Workshop

October 18, 2024 | 4:00 PM - 6:00 PM
In Person: AVW3258

Welcome to another Lab Meet! We will start by getting you up to speed with all the latest XR news and opportunities over the past few weeks, and then we will discuss project updates. Then, we'll move on to our Unity Shader Graph workshop: Have you wondered how video games can create beautiful and intricate visual effects, all while running smoothly? What’s so special about a graphics card, and why does my computer need one? We will discuss the answers to these questions while you learn the basics about computer graphics, shaders, and how they power video games. Then, you will employ those basic concepts to create your very own visual effects through Unity’s shader graph. No prior experience is required for this workshop, we welcome all who are interested! Afterward, feel free to play some Beat Saber in our lab and hang out with other club members!
